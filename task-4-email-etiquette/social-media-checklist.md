# Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

## ✅ Do's
- Think before you post and ensure your content is appropriate  
- Protect your privacy using strong passwords and security settings  
- Share only accurate and verified information  
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages  
- Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement  

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don't share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Don't spread fake news or unverified content  
- Don't engage in online arguments or abusive behavior  
- Don't post anything that could harm your reputation  
- Don't spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies
