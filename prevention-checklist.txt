 Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)

 Prevention Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps  
- Never share your OTP or passwords with anyone, regardless of their claims  
- Always verify links before clicking on them  
- Avoid sharing personal information like phone numbers, addresses, or ID details online  
- Log out from accounts when using shared devices such as college labs or cyber cafés  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or logging into sensitive accounts  

 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required when sending money  
- Carefully check payment requests, especially “collect requests”  
- Do not install unknown or screen-sharing apps suggested by strangers  
- Always verify the QR code before scanning, as it may be tampered with  

 Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
